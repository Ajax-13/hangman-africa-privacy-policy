# Hangman Africa Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Hangman Africa is a mobile word game designed for entertainment and educational purposes.

## Information We Collect

The app may collect limited information automatically through third-party services used in the app.

These may include:
- Device information
- Advertising identifiers
- App interaction data
- Crash logs

This information helps improve the game and ensure proper functionality.

## Advertising

Hangman Africa may display ads provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect and use certain information according to their own privacy policies.

You can learn more here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

## Data Usage

The collected data may be used to:

- Improve game performance
- Fix bugs and crashes
- Provide relevant advertisements
- Analyze gameplay usage

## Children's Privacy

Hangman Africa does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If such data is discovered, it will be removed immediately.

## Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services including:

- Google AdMob
- Google Play Services

These services may collect information as governed by their own privacy policies.

## Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect user data, however no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

## Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, you may contact the developer.
